Valley of Memories (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Valley of Memories mobile application.

Valley of Memories does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal or non-personal user data.

All functionality of the App operates entirely offline or locally on the user’s device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect information from users, including but not limited to:

Any content created or viewed within the App remains solely on the user’s device. Valley of Memories has no access to this data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Valley of Memories does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data collection occurs, the App is compliant with children’s privacy protections, including COPPA.

6. Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit data, there is no risk of user information being accessed, shared, or breached by the developer.
